理一下2018年M: I6的红毯&发布会的糖。

【第十次合作】

Host: This is your ninth movie with Tom Cruise, how do you see the corporation between two of you, and what is new this time as you worked with Tom?

CM: We like to describe it as one long conversation about movies that’s occasionally interrupted by production. Tom and I met, we… from the very first beginning, we started talking about movies. We’d never really had a formal conversation about what movie we were going to make. We just find ourselves making a movie before we even really formally discussed it. And I think on this one, with each movie we make, we were taking all of the knowledge of the experience we’ve learned from the previous movies and applying it. And I think one of the greatest things working with Tom is we’re always learning. We never come to a movie thinking we know what we were doing. We’re always there to discover, to learn something new.

Host: Oh actually we are looking forward for your tenth corporation. Maybe in the future. Ok the second question…

TC: We’re working on Top Gun together so. He’s…

fans screaming

CM: That’s an announcement. Right there.

TC: Yeah.

Host: That’s a big announcement actually.

【第一次合作】

Chris一路营业模式开启说着Thank you超恬静走到我们这边来签名，真的太恬静了，别人过来都叫成一团，导演过来仿佛自带消音器，比没人过来还安静🤣🤣🤣 因为我三排开外，只好自己拿Suspects，然后让亲爱的基友帮我递Valkyrie给他。

Chris签到那边，看着Valkyrie，忽然在营业模式中很安静地讲了一句，This is the first movie I made with Tom.

怎么形容呢，就好像全世界只剩下那一句话回荡在紫禁城的上空，变成一朵轻飘飘的云，随后又升空遇到冷空气化成温柔的雨，打在旧时光的芭蕉叶上。

以上经本人150mm西皮粉长焦滤镜纯天然过滤。

我当场发出破音又失态的scream: Yes you did!!!（他大概要想哪里来的奇怪粉丝）

Tom过来的时候我本来想找他也签一下Valkyrie和MI，但是四排前的一米八男粉们真的太凶悍了(:з)∠) 他笑容好灿烂，是好莱坞绝无仅有的明星了！比当晚的聚光灯还耀眼！

【第十与第一】

以及又恰好是一个人提了第十部一个人提了第一部，非常巧合的浪漫了www
